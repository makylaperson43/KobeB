Kobe Bryant

THE GREATEST OF ALL TIME!!!!

Kobe was born August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He was a professional basketball player. During his entire career he played for the Los Angeles Lakers.

What is Kobe great for?

Kobe led the Lakers to the NBA Championships 5 times. He was a 18 time all star, 12 time member of the All-Defensive team, 15 time member of All-NBA Team, 2008 MVP, 2 times NBA Finals MVP, and many more accomplishments. In 2006, Kobe scored 81 points against the Toronto Raptors.

On January 26, 2020 headed to a his daughter basketball game at his Mamba Sports Academy. Kobe, his daughter(Gianna), and 7 others were killed in a helicopter crash due to weather conditions.
